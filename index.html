Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about tub and tile reglazing and our New York City service.

What is tub and tile reglazing?

Tub and tile reglazing is the process of applying a new surface coating to an existing bathtub, tile or fixture to restore its appearance and correct surface flaws. It is a highly affordable alternative to full replacement and the results look near the same as a brand-new surface.

Can you change the color of my tiles or bathtub?

Yes. One of the great advantages of our reglazing services in NYC is that you can change the color of your fixtures and tiles without replacing them. Whether you want to modernize an outdated color or simply refresh a worn surface, we can achieve the result you want.

Is reglazing cheaper than replacing fixtures?

Yes, significantly so. A full bathroom replacement or remodel in New York City can cost thousands of dollars and take weeks of disruption. Our reglazing services deliver near-identical results for a fraction of that cost with minimal disruption to your home or business.

Do you offer commercial reglazing services in NYC?

Yes. In addition to residential services, Tub & Tile Reglazing Experts offers commercial reglazing services to ensure that property managers and business owners have beautiful bathrooms and shower stalls on site. We work with offices, hotels, apartment complexes and commercial properties throughout New York City.

What surfaces can you reglaze?

We reglaze bathtubs, bathroom tiles, shower stalls, shower pans, kitchen countertops and other bathroom fixtures. We work with porcelain, fiberglass, ceramic, acrylic and cast iron surfaces. If you are unsure whether your surface can be reglazed, call us at 212-292-8918 and we will advise you.
